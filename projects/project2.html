Favourhood

A social good app designed to promotes societal prosperity by building connection between the individuals sharing the same neighborhood.

Project type

Solo project

Roll

Ux-UI designer Researcher

Background

While doing my ux design course I was assigned a project to design a social good app.
With thousands of ways to contribute to societal good, I wanted to narrow down and select a single, yet significant way to make a positive impact and I decided to work on an app that promotes societal prosperity and fosters community building by bringing individuals sharing a neighborhood closer together."

Strategy

A Survey

To collect data on the closeness among people in a typical community

Before putting any effort towards projects objective which is to bring people in the neighborhood together I wanted to know the current condition of connection and closeness among people and interest in them to connect with each other.

I conducted survey and interviewed 21 individuals selecting typical neighborhoods which are, an apartment complex and a PG where I was residing at the time . The apartment complex was primarily occupied by families, while the PG housed male working bachelors.

Questions Asked

  1. since how long have you been staying in the current location ?

  2. on the scale of 1-10 how well do you know the people from your neighborhood ?

  3. if you have rated below 4 for previous question, briefly describe what might be the reason for it ( optional )

  4. what are the struggles that you had because of not knowing anyone in your neighborhood (optional) ?

  5. how interested you are in getting familiar with people around you ?

Analyzing

Analyzing survey data and understanding reality

The data collected from the survey has allowed me to gain a comprehensive understanding of the current state of social connections among people within the community, which can be effectively illustrated through the graphical representation presented below.

After conducting interviews with various participants, it was interesting to note that despite the majority of them having lived in the same location for a period of 6-8 months, their level of familiarity with their neighbors was surprisingly low. This finding was particularly intriguing and noteworthy, and I have visualized the data in the following graph.

Drawing conclusions

  1. significant number of individuals are not familiar with their neighbors, even though they see them daily and have been living in the same location for months or years

  2. Although no one’s putting any effort towards socializing with their neighbors the interest to do so is very high.

  3. the most common reasons people gave for not socializing with their neighbors are busy life and ‘lack of reason initiate conversation with neighbors’.

  4. The participants also highlighted that not knowing one's neighbors can lead to feelings of helplessness, loneliness, lack of confidence, and a sense of alienation and not belonging.

Identifying

identifying challenges to social prosperity and community building

After analyzing the survey data, it has become clear that people are largely disconnected from their neighbors, and the challenges that one have to face for being disconnected are many

Consequences of being disconnected

  1. when in need of little help not knowing anyone from around can complicate things

  2. not knowing one's neighbors can make one feel lonely and alienated, leading to a lack of sense of belonging.

  3. It can result in a lack of trust or understanding among community member.

  4. It can limit opportunities for social engagement and personal growth

Insights on obstacles to socialize

lack of reason to initiate conversation (explicitly mentioned)

Apart from busy life, ‘lack of reason to initiate conversation with neighbors’ is the biggest factor that’s contributing to social disconnection. walking up to neighbors house without any reason may result in awkwardness and anxiety.

Socializing is not Fun and useful

the absence of fun and usefulness element in socializing may be leading people to stay disconnected where they’d rather choose solitary entertainment like binge-watching movies or gaming instead of socializing.

Absence of dedicated channel to socialize

with Absence of dedicated channel to to socialize with neighbors, people are often limited to chance encounters and brief interactions like saying “hi good morning” when they confront their neighbor. These brief interactions may not be enough to establish meaningful connections or friendships.

Having no reason Initiate conversation

Setting requirements for potential solution

Based on the insights, I have developed a set of requirements that the solution must meet to effectively counteract the obstacles for socializing.

Requirement 1 :       solution must give an individual seeking to initiate friendship with their neighbors, a reason to approach them making the process as less anxious as possible

Requirement 2 :     solution must provide a proper platform and opportunities for people sharing a neighborhood to engage in socializing and build deeper connections with each other.

Requirement 3 :       solution must make the process of socializing use-full.

Requirement 4 :       solution must make the process of socializing Fun and Entertaining.

Brain Storming

coming up with ideas to solve the identified problems

Following a productive brainstorming session, I generated numerous ideas and carefully selected two ideas which can successfully satisfy all the requirements mentioned earlier.

1 . ‘ Favors ’ as a medium to connect people

Who doesn’t need help once in a while, all of us have experienced situations where a small act of kindness from someone could make a significant difference while doing something and my idea is design an app which uses ‘act of helping’ as a medium to bring people together.

  • This gives an individual a reason to go talk to their neighbors.

  • Makes act of socializing more use full

  • Provides opportunity for some one to establish friendship

The following storyboard illustrates the idea discussed above in action, depicting the process of using acts of helping as a means to bring people in a neighborhood together and build lasting connections.

Inspiration

my inspiration to this idea came from remembrance of my visit to my grandparents house. Unlike cities, villages lack commercial services or businesses for people to hire for various household chores. As a result, when villagers need assistance with a complex task at home, they often rely on their neighbors for help, and while working together they talk and share a lot about their day, family, events, opinions, interests etc... and I believe this sharing of thoughts brings them together.

2 . Neighborhood bonding activities

Assigning series of engaging and collaborative activities to a neighborhood can bring people together, sparks joy and encourages socializing. it offers a chance for people to step out of their daily routines and work together towards a common goal. these shared experiences, can cultivate a stronger sense of community and make lasting connections with neighbors.

  • this makes socializing more fun

  • provides a platform to socialize

Usability testing

Since the app's functionalities were fairly simple and with my cautious designing the app that i designed had '0' usability issues. this conclusion was derived on the fact that there were no participants who failed to finish the given tasks on prototype.

Privacy concerns

although there was no usability issue in the designed app, some participants expressed dissatisfaction about the privacy and personal information they have to share in the app.

User comments

  1. "Im not comfortable with sharing personal info like 'what am i doing' with supposed stranger who happened to be in my neighborhood"

  2. "I think someone can take advantage of the information of people on the app and commit fraud"

  3. " I'd definitely use the app only if it make me feel secured "

Solutions

1. include privacy settings which allows user to set who can see there post so they can alter it according to the sensitivity of the information they're about to share.
2. include strict instruction at the beginning of "ask favour" user flow to warn users about the consequences they may face in misusing the app.
