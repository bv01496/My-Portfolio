Design background

Being a social platform for students, Krayonnz has a large community of student users. With a goal of creating job opportunities for talented students, Krayonnz team decided to developing a job portal. This platform will allow employers seeking employees or interns to sign up and post their requirements, which will be responded to by students using the Krayonnz platform.
I took charge of the entire website design process, Given the fast-paced nature of Krayonnz, coupled with the tight project timeline, my primary focus was on accelerating the design process and delivering core features promptly, rather than conducting an exhaustive research on project fundamentals. To achieve this goal, I explored various existing job portals to gain insights into their functionality, user experience, and how these aspects could be effectively integrated into our own requirements.
Based on the valuable insights and research findings, I translated the requirements into user flows and wireframes. With the approval of stakeholders, I transformed the wireframes into high-fidelity mockups that showcased the finalized design concept based on those findings i created user flows and wireframes and were turned into mockups with the approval of stakeholders
